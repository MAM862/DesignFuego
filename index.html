What is good design?

“Good design,” by definition, involves several things. For me, good design requires creativity, simplicity, and aesthetics. In my experience with social media, creativity plays a key role. For example, I want to grab people’s attention and not post what everyone else posts. Of course, staying in the loop with trends is crucial, but being creative and making minor changes to trends can make one profile stand out. Simplicity keeps everything organized or “simple” while still having a wow factor to my design. Which then leads me to aesthetics or visual consistency. For example, keeping an aesthetically pleasing profile (color scheme, consistent fonts, and filters) keeps customers engaged and can easily consume my content.
